XYZ PROJECT RATIONALE
The concept of my XYZ project is surreal Chinese fortune cookie messages. This informed both the content of my posters as well as the visual identity. With each poster the viewer can refresh their webpage to show them different nonsense and surreal fortune cookie messages. The objective of the project was to design and create posters that are part designed by the designer as well as partly designed by the computer. The different messages will appear at random based on the computer's interaction/involvement with the process of generating and randomly selecting different words to inform the message. Finally, some posters are additionally designed using CSS animations and different effects to try and create posters that are more interactive to the viewer.
** XYZ Posters are only desktop friendly **
