SAGE GREEN AND WHITE + BLACK AND WHITE TOTE BAG DESIGNS

For Private Mountain's merchandise I designed a series of tote bag designs. The Pocket - Promotional Social Media Tiles

The purpose of each social media tile is to promote the DJs and artists for Miscellaneous Fuzz's social media event posts. The designs were produced in Photoshop using a high threshold technique, while artist names were created using hand-stamped lettering. Each letter was cut from thick cardboard, ink-pressed onto paper, and scanned to capture the texture of ink, paper, and material. These scanned elements were then composed in InDesign alongside the imagery to create the final layouts.
