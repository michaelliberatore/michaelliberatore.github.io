Liberative Creative is a multidisciplinary graphic design practice founded by Michael Liberatore.

Michael's practice specialises in branding, web, editorial, digital, and print-based design - where visual expression meets practical purpose.

Rooted in a DIY approach and commercial awareness, Michael's work moves between client projects and independent exploration, building visual systems that exist across both physical and digital spaces.

At its core, Liberative Creative is about creating considered & functional design that carries meaning beyond just aesthetics.
