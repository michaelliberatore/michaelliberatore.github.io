PROJECT RATIONALE
Project rationale
The theme of my ImageNet project is Cassette album art typography. I sourced from Discogs.com 30 cassette covers from three different genres, I extracted from the 30 cassette album art covers interesting contrasting typography.The extracted typography has been categorised in three categories which are 'Punk', 'Psychedelic', and 'Reggae'. My process was sourcing and downloading different album art covers which are both old and new from Discogs.com and simply extracting in Photoshop different typographic elements and words to showcase in my project. All of the words and images are organised according to their contrasting colours. My design consists of a simple design incorporating complementary colours to further reinforce my idea of contrasting typography within the album art covers. Through using CSS I have additionally created my project to be as interactive to the user as possible. With the navigation bar scrolling down with the user, all of my images using a hover function so the complementary colours are revealed when the user hovers their mouse over each individual image, and having my website be compatable with smartphone screens. This project will hopefully serve as an interesting archive into obscure cassette album art as well as interesting typography.
