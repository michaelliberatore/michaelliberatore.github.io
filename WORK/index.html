PRIVATE MOUNTAIN - TOTE BAG DESIGNS

Tote bag designs for Private Mountain, translating their VHS/glitch aesthetic into a minimal, print-ready form. The work uses subtle texture and distortion inspired by CRT and analogue TV imagery, and was screen printed onto AS Colour tote bags.

All tote bags were printed by Sound Merch

The Pocket - Promotional Social Media Tiles

Social media tiles promoting DJs and artists for Miscellaneous Fuzz event 'The Pocket'. Imagery Created in Photoshop using a high-threshold technique, with hand-stamped lettering made from inked cardboard cuts, scanned to retain a DIY, tactile texture. Final layouts were composed in InDesign with accompanying imagery.
