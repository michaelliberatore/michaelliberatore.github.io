PRIVATE MOUNTAIN - TOTE BAG DESIGNS

Tote bag designs for Private Mountain, translating their VHS aesthetic into a minimal, print-ready form. The work uses subtle texture and distortion inspired by CRT and analogue TV imagery, and was screen printed onto AS Colour tote bags.

All tote bags were printed by Sound Merch
EMILY LUMSDEN PHOTOGRAPHY BRANDING


A complete brand refresh for photographer Emily Lumsden, designed to bring greater cohesion across both digital and printed touchpoints while reflecting the timeless elegance of her work.

The project included the design of a custom logo and monogram, printed business cards, and a large-format A2 studio poster, creating a refined and consistent visual identity across all brand materials.

A rewarding collaboration that helped translate Emily's distinctive photographic style into a thoughtful and enduring brand presence.
CORAL CAVES - SUNSET FIRE EP

Debut EP artwork for Coral Caves' Sunset Fire.

Developed through a hands-on and collaborative process, the project explored themes of coastal nostalgia & textures.

The visual identity centred around a custom typeface designed specifically for the project, paired with analogue photography to create a tactile and authentic aesthetic.

The Pocket - Promotional Social Media Tiles

Social media tiles promoting DJs and artists for Miscellaneous Fuzz's event 'The Pocket'. Imagery Created in Photoshop using a high-threshold technique, with hand-stamped lettering made from inked cardboard cut outs, scanned to retain a DIY, tactile texture. Final layouts were composed in InDesign with accompanying imagery.

WEDDING INVITATION SUITE


Wedding invitation suite designed for Nicholas and Christina Di Sisto, printed on 300gsm Splendorgel Cream stock with gold foil detailing.

A special project that hit close to home, this invitation suite was created for my sister and brother-in-law's wedding. The suite included a main Wedding invitation, RSVP card, and wishing well card, combining classic typography with premium gold foiling print finishes.

Printed & foiled by Paperpoint Australia, whose exceptional print quality and gold foiling brought the design to life.

STUDIO ANGEL LOGO & BRANDING


Logo design for fine art photographer and videographer Studio Angel.

The new identity is inspired by the raw, nostalgic, and often hazy texture of Super 8 film, reflecting the studio’s cinematic approach to image-making.

The type choice draws from film subtitles, reinforcing a sense of vintage storytelling and analogue film.
